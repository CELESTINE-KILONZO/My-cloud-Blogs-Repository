**Aws party rock is a platform that lets users create AI-powered apps using generative AI without the need to code inorder to come up with an application**. It's based on Amazon Bedrock. 

**Features**

* **One can build an app by just explaining what they need in their app**
    
    * **one can share the app with other by using a public link.Private ink is still available**
        
        * **widgets are used to create the app**
            
        * one can update their own documents from their local machine
